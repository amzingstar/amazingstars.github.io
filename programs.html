Programs and Daily Activities

We combine learning, movement, and creativity in a structured home daycare routine.

Early Learning

Alphabet, numbers, colors, and shape recognition through songs, books, and games.

Creative Play

Art projects, building activities, role play, and sensory toys for imagination and problem solving.

Outdoor Time

Daily active play in a backyard setup with slide, swings, and supervised movement games.

Social Growth

Sharing, communication, and friendship-building in a caring and supportive environment.
