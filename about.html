About Amazing Stars Kids Care

Amazing Stars Kids Care is a warm, nurturing home daycare where children are encouraged to learn, grow, and shine every day. We provide a safe, loving, and engaging environment that feels like a second home for your child.

Families choose Amazing Stars Kids Care because of the strong focus on trust, open communication, and creating a warm atmosphere where children are not just cared for but truly supported to shine.

Learning Through Play

We follow a thoughtfully designed early learning curriculum that supports:

  • Cognitive development
  • Language and communication skills
  • Social and emotional growth
  • Creativity and imagination

Children learn best through play, exploration, music, stories, and hands-on activities. Our daily routine balances learning, free play, outdoor time, rest, and nutritious meals to support healthy development.

Our Mission

To create a loving home environment where children learn through play, build friendships, and develop confidence every day.

Why Families Choose Us

Small group setting, personalized attention, clean and organized spaces, and daily activities focused on learning and fun.

What your child will experience

  • Colorful classroom space for reading, shapes, colors, and circle time
  • Outdoor play area with slides, swings, and open movement
  • Age-appropriate toys and hands-on creative play
  • A kind, nurturing routine that supports social and emotional development
